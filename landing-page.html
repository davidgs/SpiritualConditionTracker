Track Your Spiritual Fitness

The Spiritual Condition Tracker is a mobile app designed specifically for individuals in Alcoholics Anonymous, providing comprehensive digital tools to support your personal growth and recovery journey.

Meeting Check-ins

Record your attendance at meetings and keep a detailed history of your participation in your recovery community.

Prayer & Meditation Tracking

Log your daily prayer and meditation practice to maintain mindfulness of your spiritual development.

Literature Reading

Track time spent reading AA literature and other recovery materials to strengthen your knowledge and understanding.

Sponsor Interactions

Document your interactions with sponsors and sponsees, fostering accountability and connection.

Spiritual Fitness Score

View your calculated Spiritual Fitness score based on your tracked activities, helping you assess your overall recovery health.

Privacy-Focused

All your data is stored locally on your device, ensuring complete privacy and control over your personal information.
